For Citizens (Users)

What is this app?
A public platform for free delivery and local food operated by Gyeongsan City.
Is delivery really free?
Yes. Participating merchants offer free delivery. (Some conditions may apply)
Are there any fees?
No commission fees for both citizens and merchants - 0 won.
What stores can I order from?
You can order from various merchants in Gyeongsan including restaurants, traditional markets, and local food stores.
Can I order from multiple traditional market stores at once?
Yes! You can order from multiple stores like side dish shops, butchers, and fruit shops in one order with bundled delivery.
How is local food different?
Fresh produce harvested the same day is sold directly by producers, and you can verify the producer's name and farm location.
Can non-Gyeongsan residents use this?
Yes. Tourists visiting Gyeongsan can also use it, and specialty product delivery service is available.
How do I use local currency?
Additional discounts are provided when paying with local currency like Gyeongsan Pay.
How long does delivery take?
Regular delivery is same-day, local food is next-day delivery by default. (May vary by product)
How do I cancel an order?
Free cancellation is available before product shipment. Press the cancel button in 'Order History' in the app.
Where do I contact if there's a problem?
Contact 'Customer Service' in the app or Gyeongsan City Hall (â˜Ž 053-810-XXXX).
Is it easy for seniors to use?
Yes. Senior-friendly design with large fonts, simple screens, and voice guidance is applied.
Is sign-up complicated?
No. Sign-up is completed in 1 minute with just mobile phone verification.
Are there any coupons or benefits?
Various benefits including new member coupons, tourist special coupons, and additional local currency discounts.
Will it expand to other regions?
Yes. After the Gyeongsan pilot, it will expand to Pohang, Gumi, Andong, and throughout Gyeongbuk.

For Merchants

Is there a franchise fee?
No. Franchise fees, commission fees, and advertising costs are all 0 won.
How do I apply?
Just take one photo of your business registration certificate in the app and complete application in 3 minutes.
How do I register the menu?
You can register the menu later after application. Staff will assist you.
Can I register on-site?
Yes. Staff at the traditional market on-site booth will take photos and register for you.
When will I be approved?
Automatic approval within 24 hours of application. (We'll contact you if there are document issues)
Who does the delivery?
Gyeongsan City designated delivery agency delivers for free. (Delivery fee is supported by the city)
What is bundled delivery?
A method of collecting orders from multiple stores within the traditional market and delivering them at once.
When do I receive settlement?
Weekly settlement is automatically deposited to your registered account.
The photo came out blurry.
That's okay. The administrator will check and contact you to retake or manually input.
Can traditional market merchants participate?
Yes! There's a preferential policy for traditional market merchants, and group registration by merchant associations is possible.
Are there special benefits on market days?
Yes. On market days, a 'Market Day Special' banner is automatically displayed, bringing more orders.
Can I register multiple stores at once?
Yes. You can register multiple locations if they are under your name.
Can I modify information after registration?
Yes. You can modify anytime in the 'Merchant Management' menu in the app.
Is there a subscription fee or monthly fee?
Not at all. All costs from sign-up to operation are 0 won.
How do I unsubscribe if I want to?
You can apply for withdrawal in the app anytime or contact customer service for processing.

No. You can register anytime at your convenience. No need to rush.
Can staff register for me?

Administration/Operations/Trust

Can unauthorized registration be prevented?
Yes. The existence of business registration numbers is immediately verified through the National Tax Service API.
Is this really a public app?
Yes. It's an official public platform directly operated by Gyeongsan City Hall.
Is personal information safe?
Yes. We comply with the Personal Information Protection Act and public institution security standards, with encrypted storage.
Why create this when private delivery apps exist?
To solve the problem of high commission fees burdening small businesses and lack of publicness.
Will it expand to other regions in the future?
Yes. After the Gyeongsan pilot operation, phased expansion throughout Gyeongbuk including Pohang, Gumi, and Andong is planned.
